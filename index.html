DIY Sushi at Home

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish centered around vinegared rice combined with seafood, vegetables, or egg, often served with wasabi, soy sauce, and pickled ginger.

Sushi with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger 🍣

These are ingredients on how to make a modern rice sushi

  1. Seaweed

  2. Short-grain rice with rice vinegar, sugar, and salt

  3. Seaweed sheets to roll into sushi roll

  4. bamboo mat (Nori mat)

  5. Fish such as salmon, tuna, and yellow-tail

  6. Vegetable like avocado or cucumber

  7. you can also have soy sauce, wasabi, or pickled ginger

This is how to make your sushi

  1. Gather all your ingredients listed up on top

  2. Get your nori mat and put your seaweed on the mat then add a thin layer of your seasoned rice on the seaweed, add your fillings in the middle.

  3. Then wrap your sushi with the nori mat and carefully cut your sushi.

  4. Now you can serve this delicious food with some soy sauce, pickled ginger, and wasabi.

Enjoy 😋.
