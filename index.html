Sushi

DIY Sushi at Home

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish centered around vinegared rice combined with seafood, vegetables, or egg, often served with wasabi, soy sauce, and pickled ginger.

Sushi with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger 🍣

Ingredients for Modern Rice Sushi

  1. Seaweed sheets (Nori)
  2. Short-grain rice (seasoned with rice vinegar, sugar, and salt)
  3. Bamboo rolling mat (Makisu)
  4. Fresh fish (such as salmon, tuna, and yellowtail)
  5. Vegetables (like avocado or cucumber)
  6. Condiments (soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger)

How to Make Your Sushi

  1. Gather all your ingredients listed above.
  2. Place your seaweed sheet on the bamboo mat, then spread a thin, even layer of seasoned rice over the seaweed. Leave a small gap at the top edge.
  3. Add your chosen fillings horizontally across the center of the rice.
  4. Roll the sushi tightly using the bamboo mat, seal the edge with a dab of water, and carefully cut into bite-sized pieces using a sharp, wet knife.
  5. Serve your fresh creation with soy sauce, pickled ginger, and wasabi!

Enjoy 😋
